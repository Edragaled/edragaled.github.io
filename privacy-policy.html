Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 13, 2026

1. Collection of Your Information

When you play Pixel Chronicles, we may collect:

  • Account identifiers (PlayFab ID, device ID, or Google Play Games ID)
  • Gameplay data (progression, inventory, achievements, guild membership)
  • Chat messages (global chat, guild chat, private messages)
  • Purchase information (in-app purchases validated via PlayFab and platform stores)

We do not collect passwords or sensitive personal information such as government IDs.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and improve gameplay features
  • Enable multiplayer chat and guild systems
  • Validate purchases and deliver in-game items
  • Ensure fair play and prevent abuse
  • Maintain account security and integrity

3. Data Sharing

We may share data with trusted third-party services used to operate the Game:

  • PlayFab (account, inventory, purchases)
  • Azure Services (SignalR chat, cloud functions)
  • Google Play / Apple App Store (purchase validation)

We do not sell or share your data with advertisers.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Since we do not store personal data directly, deletion requests must be handled through the services that process your data.

Game progress: Uninstalling the game removes local data from your device.

You may request deletion of any data linked to your device identifier or purchases by contacting us directly. We will forward your request to the appropriate providers.

👉 To request deletion of your data, please contact us at: support@moonforge-games.com
